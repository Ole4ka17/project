Fun facts

  1. Nevada has 314 named mountain ranges — more mountain ranges than any other state — and 35 peaks over 10,000 feet in elevation. Because of the dramatic geography, the state has many pockets of unique habitat for plants and wildlife to call home.
  2. Nevada ranks 8th for the number of butterflies found in a single state.
  3. Nevada has 309 species which are not found anywhere else, including desert fish like the Devil’s hole pupfish and desert dace, a variety springsnails and amphibians like the adorable Dixie Valley toad.
  4. With over 300 hot springs, Nevada is home to more hot springs than any other state in the nation.
  5. 15 of the top 25 largest hotels in the world reside in Las Vegas.
  6. The state is home to the Berlin-Ichthyosaur Park, the only place with a complete skeleton of an ichthyosaur, an extinct marine reptile.
